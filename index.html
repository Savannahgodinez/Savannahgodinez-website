Cosmetologist

Growing up makeup and hair was something that always kept me entertained as a child and it brought me a lot of joy. As I grew up I still continued to really enjoy it and like it a lot. So I decided to pursue it as a career and make it into a job.I began learning how to do hair and makeup on other people to gain practie and I created an instagram to keep it as a portfolio. As time went on I eventually started to gain some clientele. That was huge for me and that was when I really decided to take into condsideration being a full time cosmetologist. So during my senior year of high school I got into and joined a ROP cosmetology program and began the process of getting my license. The requirements were a bit toughf but I managed and I am currently almost half way through my hours. We need 1600 hours of hands on cosmetology practices before we can go to statedboard and take the test to get our license.

Although it is a tough endeavour to achieve I still decided to follow through with it even if it meant losing out on some senior activites. Till this day I consider it one of the best decisions I have made for myself simply because it allows me to do what I love as my job. As an adult that is truly something I wanted for myself. I would definitely recommend it to anyone who has the same interests as me.
